Hi, I'm Riley.

I am a mutual aid farmer, FPV drone pilot, and a migrant rights activist. I also write software.

I believe that free and open source software is a profound and radical movement among software engineers which can transform how we organize and mobilize, but the digital is useful only to the extent that it facilitates the material.

We cannot supplement community with technology, we must organize first for the liberation of all peoples and optimize these processes second.
