what is often overlooked is that C^* algebras need a norm defined

we can always make a hilbert space out of that norm, but thats not what we want

We can start there and make a hilbert space and then restrict to bounded guys
thats all cool but then once we define entropy there, we don't know any relation
between entropy defined in this hilb. and any other hilb. that can be constructed.
